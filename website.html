How to bake a cake
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup oil or butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
Equipment:
  • Pressure cooker (no gasket/whistle)
  • Wire rack or ring
  • Greased cake tin
Steps:
  • Preheat cooker (no water) on medium for 5 mins.
  • Mix dry and wet ingredients separately, then combine.
  • Pour into tin, place on rack inside cooker.
  • Cover and bake on low heat for 30–45 mins.
  • Check with toothpick; if clean, it's ready.

How to make cold coffee

Cold Coffee Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1–2 tsp instant coffee
  • 1–2 tbsp sugar
  • 4–5 ice cubes
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream (optional)
  • 2 tbsp cold water

Instructions:

  1. Mix coffee, sugar, and water.
  2. Blend with milk, ice, and ice cream (if using).
  3. Serve chilled. Optional: top with cream or syrup.

How to make hot tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tea bag or 1 tsp loose tea
  • 1–2 tsp sugar or honey (optional)
  • Milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a pot or kettle.
  2. Add tea and steep for 2–5 minutes.
  3. Add sugar or honey to taste.
  4. Add milk if desired. Serve hot.
